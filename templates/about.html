About This App

  • This app allows users to upload audio files of meetings and generates a text summary of the meeting notes.

  • It's designed to help professionals save time by providing quick and accurate summaries of their meetings.

  • The app uses advanced audio processing and natural language processing techniques to transcribe and summarize the audio content.

  • Key Features:
    • Accurate Transcriptions: Utilizes state-of-the-art speech recognition technology to ensure high accuracy in transcriptions.
    • Automatic Summarization: Generates concise summaries that capture the key points and action items from your meetings.
    • User-Friendly Interface: Simple and intuitive interface that allows users to upload and manage their audio files effortlessly.
